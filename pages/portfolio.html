Hi, I'm Sabrina

I've been designing and producing merch since 2013. I used to be a webcomic artist - now I'm passionate about telling stories through product designs that are unique and charming.

My portfolio features both my own original characters and unlicensed fanart. All designs are mine unless otherwise stated.

"Packintosh" Ita Bag Concept
2020
Original concept for a convertible "ita" backpack/purse themed around vintage PCs. Removable inserts featured different applications and desktop backgrounds for customers to showcase interests. Would come with a set of 3 enamel pins (cursor, file-folder, recycle-bin) to represent icons on a desktop.
